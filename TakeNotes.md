## Math Terms
**Standard Deviation** (aka sigma, SD, root mean squared error 标准偏差): 
> $$sd = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n({actual - pred})^2/n}$$

e.g.
> math.sqrt(np.mean(np.power(y_test - preds, 2)))
> math.sqrt(sklearn.metrics.mean_squared_error(y_test, preds))
